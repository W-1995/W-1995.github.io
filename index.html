Introduction

The existing benchmarks do not include enough wild-animal-sequence to evaluate the performance of the tracker. So we build the first benchmark for wild animal tracking(WATB).
 Our tracking benchmarks include：
  • Comprises 206 sequences.
  • Covers different kinds of animals from land, sea and sky.
  • The total frames of WATB are over 203,000.
  • the average sequence length is over 980 frames.
  • All the sequences are labeled with 13 challenge attributes.
If you want more information about WATB, Please check out the benchmark details at WATB Benchmark page, evaluation toolkit and sample results at Evaluation and Result.

Video

We selected three labeled video sequences from 206 data sets for display. These three videos represent sea, land and air respectively. These datasets are annotated manually, and then the annotated datasets are reviewed. For more data set sequences, please see Benchmark page.
