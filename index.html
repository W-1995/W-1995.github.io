Introduction

We propose the first benchmark, named Wild Animal Tracking Benchmark (WATB), to encourage further progress of research and applications of visual object tracking in wildlife protection.
 Our tracking benchmarks include：
  • Comprises 206 sequences.
  • Covers different kinds of animals from land, sea and sky.
  • The total frames of WATB are over 203,000.
  • the average sequence length is over 980 frames.
  • All the sequences are labeled with 13 challenge attributes.
If you want more information about WATB, Please check out the benchmark details at WATB Benchmark page, evaluation toolkit and sample results at Evaluation.
